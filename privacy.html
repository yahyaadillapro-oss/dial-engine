Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 12, 2026

DIAL Engine is a lightweight CRM solution designed to operate natively within the Google Drive ecosystem. Your privacy and data security are our top priorities.

1. Data Collection & Storage

DIAL Engine does not collect, store, or transmit your spreadsheet data to any external servers. All lead information, contact details, and call logs remain strictly within your own Google Workspace environment.

2. App Permissions

The system requires access to your Google Sheets solely to:

  • Automate row navigation for faster dialing.
  • Update call statuses in real-time.
  • Link worker sheets to the Master KPI Dashboard via secure internal triggers.

3. Third-Party Access

We do not share, sell, or provide access to your data to any third parties. Since there is no browser extension or external database, your information is protected by Google’s enterprise-grade security.
