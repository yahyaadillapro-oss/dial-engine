PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: March 12, 2026

DIAL ENGINE is a native Google Drive CRM. We operate under a "Zero-Footprint" philosophy, ensuring your data never leaves your personal ecosystem.

1. Data Sovereignty

We do not host your data. All property information from Zillow and lead details from your VOIP-ready sheets stay within your private Google Workspace. We have zero access to your spreadsheets.

2. App Logic & Triggers

Our custom Apps Script logic runs locally within your account. The "Master KPI Injection" feature only transmits status updates between sheets you authorize. No third-party servers are involved.

3. Security

By eliminating browser extensions, DIAL ENGINE removes the risk of credential scraping or background data collection common in traditional CRMs.
